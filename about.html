NCC Emblem

The National Cadet Corps

The National Cadet Corps is India’s premier uniformed youth organization, nurturing discipline, leadership, and service among students across the country. It brings together motivated cadets from schools and colleges into a tri-services environment shaped by the Army, Navy, and Air Force ethos.

At BMSIT&M, the NCC unit upholds the motto "Unity and Discipline" through structured training, parade practice, and community outreach aligned with national values.

The Three Aims of NCC

01

Character and Discipline

Develop cadets with strong character, comradeship, and a disciplined approach to leadership and service.

02

Leadership-Ready Youth

Create a trained and motivated youth force capable of taking leadership roles in all walks of life.

03

Service Motivation

Motivate young people to take up careers in the Armed Forces and serve the nation with pride.
