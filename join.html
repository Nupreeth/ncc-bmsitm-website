Why Join NCC

Certificate Value

B and C Certificates offer grace marks, bonus points in civil services, and preferred status in defence recruitment.

Camp Experiences

National camps (RDC, AITSC, Para Basic, CATC) take you across India with unmatched exposure.

Life Skills

Leadership, discipline, teamwork, time management, and public speaking forged through real training.

Eligibility

  • Who can join: 1st year BE students at BMSIT&M.
  • Age limit: Up to 26 years.
  • Process: Written test + Physical fitness test.
  • Enrollment period: Every October.
  • Strength: 52 cadets (authorised). Limited spots.

What Happens After You Join

  1. Enrollment & Oath Taking.
  2. Weekly parade training and drill.
  3. Theory subjects: map reading, weapon training, first aid.
  4. Camp selections for CATC, AITSC, and Pre-RDC.
  5. Certificate examinations (B and C).
  6. Appointments and honours for top cadets.

FAQ

Is NCC compulsory at BMSIT?

No, NCC is optional and open to all eligible students.

Can girls join?

Yes, NCC is open to all genders.

What is the time commitment?

Weekly parade and periodic camp duty.

Does NCC affect studies?

Camp leave is officially permitted. The ANO coordinates with departments.

What are the benefits of a C Certificate?

Bonus marks in UPSC/civil services and relaxation in Armed Forces recruitment eligibility.
