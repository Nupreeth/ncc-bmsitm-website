Unit Timeline

A decade of discipline, growth, and national presence.

2016

  • Unit established at BMSIT&M.
  • Orientation and first cadet enrollments.
  • Initial parade drills commence.

2017-18

  • First CATC at Gopallan Grounds and Gitam University.
  • B Certificate exam 2018.

2018-19

  • CATC at Christ University.
  • Republic Day and Independence Day parades.
  • Invited talk by Major Gen. Bhakuni VSM on SSB.

2019-20

  • RDC 2020 representation.
  • CATC at NCC Academy Hosahalli.
  • Talk by Rear Admiral G. Srinivasan on joining the Armed Forces.

2020-21

  • Republic Day 2021 activities.
  • Fit India Freedom Run, One Student One Tree.
  • COVID-era awareness and vaccination drives.

2021-22

  • Rajpath Parade 2021 — CSUO Deeksha Sethi, Silver Medal, All India Best Cadet.
  • Blood Donation Camp (400+ units).
  • Rank piping ceremonies and appointments.

2022-23

  • B and C Certificate exams.
  • Combined Annual Training Cadre continuation.

2023-24

  • First AITSC representation — CSUO Pooja P, Bronze in Map Reading.
  • RDC and innovation-linked achievements.

2024-25

  • DG Felicitation Program and Aero India Show.
  • C and B Certificate exams.
  • Farewell for 2021-24 and 2022-25 batches.

2025-26

  • CATC-II / TSC-I awards: Best GoH, Best Camp Senior.
  • AITSC 2025 podium (Map Reading 2nd).
  • Para Basic 2025 — Best SD Cadet.
  • Republic Day 2026, Annual Review / GC Visit, CM & DDG Commendations.
