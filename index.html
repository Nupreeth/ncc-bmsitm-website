About the Unit

The unit operates as 39/17 NCC Company under 39 Karnataka Battalion, Bangalore B Group, Karnataka and Goa Directorate. The unit is based at BMSIT&M and trains cadets in parade discipline, map reading, weapon training, and camp progression.

With an authorised strength of 52 cadets, the unit has achieved national representation through RDC, AITSC, TSC, and Para Basic placements.

The Aims of NCC

01

Character and Discipline

Develop character, comradeship, discipline, leadership, and the spirit of selfless service.

02

Leadership-Ready Youth

Create organized, trained, and motivated youth capable of leading in all walks of life.

03

Service Motivation

Provide an environment that motivates young people to take up careers in the Armed Forces.
